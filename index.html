About the studio

Based in Mwea, working across Kenya.

MONA Studio is a professional photography and creative studio established in 2021. Based in Mwea, Kirinyaga County, we provide high-quality photography services across Kenya. We are committed to creativity, professionalism and exceptional customer service.

Mission

To provide creative, high-quality photography that preserves memories and exceeds client expectations.

Vision

To become one of Kenya's leading creative studios, recognized for quality, innovation and professionalism.
